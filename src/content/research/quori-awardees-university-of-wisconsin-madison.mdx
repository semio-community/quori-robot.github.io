## Project Overview

The University of Wisconsin-Madison team develops foundational building blocks for autonomous social robots by combining formal methods, emotionally sophisticated interaction design, and a comprehensive HRI software stack.

## Research Thrusts

- Integrating formal methods into human-robot interaction design
- Enabling emotionally sophisticated human-robot interactions
- Developing a comprehensive software stack for HRI
