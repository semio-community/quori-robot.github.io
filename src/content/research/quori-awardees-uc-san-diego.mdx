## Project Overview

The UC San Diego team advances core robotics technology for Quori, spanning human-centered motion planning, machine learning, and systems engineering for long-term deployments in home and health settings.

## Research Focus

The project emphasizes onboard sensing, computation, and unsupervised learning for autonomous social robots, with application domains that include community health and dementia care.
